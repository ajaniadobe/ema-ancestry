    Featured AncestryDNA Articles

    Reading Your Ethnicity Estimate

    DNA ancestral region results are reports that use DNA testing to provide information about a person's ancestral origins. AncestryDNA analyzes a person's genetic makeup and compares it to reference populations from various regions around the world to estimate the person's genetic origins.
    Find out more ...

    AncestryDNA Test Accuracy and Precision

    Your AncestryDNA test results are the product of a multi-step process. There is no single measure of AncestryDNA test accuracy. Instead, the accuracy and precision of each step can be measured independently.
    Read more about test accuracy

    How does the AncestryDNA test differ from other DNA tests?

    AncestryDNA uses an autosomal DNA test that surveys a person’s entire genome at over 700,000 locations. It covers both the maternal and paternal sides of the family tree.
    Read more about AncestryDNA

    Types of DNA Testing

    There are multiple types of DNA tests. The most common ones are Y-DNA testing, autosomal DNA testing, and mitochondrial DNA testing, (mtDNA testing). To understand their differences, it can help to know how DNA is organized.
    Learn more about DNA Tests
